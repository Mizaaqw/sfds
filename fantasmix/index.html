The Fabulous Blob

The blob fish is an adorable undersea animal looking for some friends! Learn more about them below!

6 Amazing Blob Fish Facts
    Blobfish grow to about 12 inches long. Wow! That's a whole foot long!


    Blobfish are from 3000 feet under the ocean!

    The flesh of the blobfish is primarily gelatinous

    The blobfish mostly eats crabs, sea urchins, and shellfish.

    Blobfish do not have bones or teeth!!!!!!!!!!!111

    Blobfish are commonly found in the oceans surrounding Australia and New Zealand
